Who are we?

Creating content people ﬁnd memorable and enjoyable is no small thing. I want to give people the ability to create the content they want to see and the ability to bring things to life with their own hands. The culture in technology is changing and more and more people are creating their own content for their own platforms but often without expertise or ﬁnesse. We want to empower people to gain those skills and create the content they have in their minds. We aim to innovate and use our brains to not only create good content, but a culture surrounding it that pushes boundaries, limits & creative ideas.
